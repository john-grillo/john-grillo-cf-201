John Andrew Grillo made this page to get into the 301 section of CodeFellows developer bootcamp.

So why then, is it so darn ugly? The answer to that one is simple: He ran out of time!

He spent much of the self-study time helping an aunt with cancer move nortward while juggling client work to pay his bills on time.

John Grillo also read the requirements list, saying that this was mainly about permanently re-writing the innerHTML of the page using javascript and a guessing game

So John Grillo wrote the website to spec. It uses HTML5, CSS3 and vanilla JavaScript to make a quick, cheap site and a guessing game

Just so he can pass the course and then move on to the 301 and finally the 401 section.

John Andrew Grillo always thinks its a good idea to review the basics. If you only get 10% new stuff, that's still 10% more than you had before.

Again, not the world's prettiest site, I know I can do better. Let's review the guidelines:
